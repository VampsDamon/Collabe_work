The Indian Army is the land-based branch of the Indian Armed Forces. It is the world's second-largest standing army and the largest army.

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks as the third most powerful air force in the world

The Indian Navy is a multi-dimensional force that has been organized to safeguard India's maritime territorial integrity and other maritime interests.
