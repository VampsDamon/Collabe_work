Indian Air Force has released the notification for recruitment under the Indian Airforce Agniveer Scheme. The candidates who desire to apply for recruitment under this Scheme need to go through a multi-stage recruitment process. The Indian Airforce Agniveer Recruitment process has three phases i.e. an online written exam, physical fitness exam, and medical test. It is mandatory to clear each level of the recruitment process separately. In the physical fitness tests the candidates have to perform certain exercises like run, push-ups, sit-ups, etc. in the given time. The candidates who clear the physical fitness test will have to undergo Adaptability Tests. This is a golden chance for all those candidates who want to join the Defence Forces and serve the country. Furthermore, this recruitment is in addition to the other Defence Exams for the recruitment in the Armed Forces.

The applicants will have to go through various levels of the selection process before they can be selected as an Agniveervayu. Only those applicants who pass the online test will be called for the physical fitness test. After clearing the Physical Fitness Test, the candidates will have to pass the Adaptability Test - I, and the Adaptability Test - II, individually. Candidates clearing all these tests will be selected for the medical examination.

The various levels of the selection procedure are given below:

  • Level 1: Online Test
  • Level 2: 
    • Physical Fitness Test
    • Adaptability Test - I
    • Adaptability Test - II
  • Level 3: Medical Examination

Important Points for Indian Airforce Agniveer Physical Fitness Test

The candidates should remember a few points before they go for the Indian Airforce Agniveer Physical Test.

  • The candidate has to pass all the levels of the Physical Fitness Test phase in order to be considered for medical examination.
  • The candidates have to keep with them the original documents like mark sheets, caste certificates, etc. and the original admit card of the online test when they report for the Physical Test. These will be checked before the start of the physical test.
  • The center for Physical Fitness Test will be allocated by the Indian Air Force, and any request for change of center will not be entertained.
  • The candidates shortlisted for the Indian Airforce Agniveer Physical Test will get a fresh admit card for the physical fitness test.
  • In case of permanent tattoos on the body, the candidate has to submit 2 photographs with details of the size and type of the tattoo.
  • The candidates should carry their sports shoes and tracks for the physical test.
  • Only those candidates who pass the physical fitness test, adaptability test-I and adaptability test-II along with document verification will be called for the medical examination.

Agneepath/Agniveer Yojna FAQs
  • What are Agneepath scheme Details?

    The Agneepath Army Bharti Scheme is a programme offered by the Indian government to all applicants. One can join the combat forces through Agnipath, and every year, 45,000 to 50,000 soldiers will be recruited for a four-year commitment.

  • What is the eligibility for the Agneepath scheme?

    The Agnipath scheme was launched by the Indian government to hire individuals for the Army, Navy, and Airforce. The process of choosing the candidates is centralised and completely open. Candidates for the Indian Army Agnipath Scheme must be between the ages of 17.5 and 21.

  • What is Agneepath scheme salary?

    Under this scheme, soldiers will receive a salary with an increase in their salary every year. After joining as Agniveer candidates will get Rs 30000 month for the first year & in last year it will increase to 40000.

  • What is Agneepath Agniveer scheme?

    The Indian Armed Forces will enrol chosen applicants as Agniveers for a four-year period as part of the central government’s Agneepath/Agnipath Scheme. 46,000 Agniveers will be recruited by the Armed Forces this year through the Agnipath/Agneepath scheme.

  • Is Agneepath Army Scheme for Girls?

    Yes, both male and female candidates can apply through the Agneepath scheme to join Indian Air Force, Army, and Navy. The Agneepath scheme eligibility for females is the same as that of a male candidate. Major Incidents in Indian History
