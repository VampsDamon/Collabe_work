Tattoo Specifications

The candidates are advised to see if they can have a tattoo on their body or not as per the notification issued. The details of who can have tattoo are given below:

  • Candidates belonging to tribal communities/from tribal areas, as declared by the Government of India Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes are permitted to have permanent body tattoos on any part of the body, as per existing customs and traditions. They will be eligible for selection only after producing “Self Certification Certificate”, “Certificate of belonging to a tribal community and permissibility of permanent body tattoo”.
  • Permanent body tattoos are permitted on the following body parts only i.e “Inner face of forearms i.e. from inside of the elbow to the wrist of both the hands” and “Reverse side of the palm/back (dorsal) side of both hands”, for which a candidate will be required to sign a Self Certification Certificate
  • Any candidate with a permanent body tattoo(s) on any part of the body other than that specified will not be eligible for enrolment into the armed forces.

Agneepath/Agniveer Yojna FAQs
  • What are Agneepath scheme Details?

    The Agneepath Army Bharti Scheme is a programme offered by the Indian government to all applicants. One can join the combat forces through Agnipath, and every year, 45,000 to 50,000 soldiers will be recruited for a four-year commitment.

  • What is the eligibility for the Agneepath scheme?

    The Agnipath scheme was launched by the Indian government to hire individuals for the Army, Navy, and Airforce. The process of choosing the candidates is centralised and completely open. Candidates for the Indian Army Agnipath Scheme must be between the ages of 17.5 and 21.

  • What is Agneepath scheme salary?

    Under this scheme, soldiers will receive a salary with an increase in their salary every year. After joining as Agniveer candidates will get Rs 30000 month for the first year & in last year it will increase to 40000.

  • What is Agneepath Agniveer scheme?

    The Indian Armed Forces will enrol chosen applicants as Agniveers for a four-year period as part of the central government’s Agneepath/Agnipath Scheme. 46,000 Agniveers will be recruited by the Armed Forces this year through the Agnipath/Agneepath scheme.

  • Is Agneepath Army Scheme for Girls?
